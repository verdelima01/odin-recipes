AeroPress Espresso

Description:

James Hoffmann won the 2007 World Barista Championship and founded Square Mile Coffee Roasters.
This is his method for mimicking an espresso using an AeroPress.

Elements involved:

  • AeroPress brewer (you cannot make this recipe without it) and paper filter
  • Adjustable coffee grinder
  • Freshly roasted coffee of your choice
  • 90g of hot water (not tab water please!)
  • Mug
  • Timer and scale

Steps:

  1. Place a paper filter in the cap and rinse with hot water
  2. Grind 18g of coffee very fine (a bit coarse than regular espresso), and add to infusion chamber (inverted method)
  3. Add 90g of hot water (91Â°C) to infusion chamber
  4. Give it an agressive stir to eliminate lumps and start timer
  5. Close cap and let it rest for 1:30min
  6. Turn the AeroPress and place it on the mug. Press slowly until you hear the hiss.
  7. Enjoy your delicious espresso!
  8. Don't be a piggy, clean the stuff!
