Pan con Tomate

Description:

Sometimes the simplest meals are the best meals. With only 4 ingredients,
this recipe will make you taste the greatness of catalan cuisine.

A large tomato, two slices of crusty bread, a garlic clove and extra virgin
olive oil (utterly important, "green" variety for the best). That's all you need.
Add salt and black pepper -freshly ground- to taste. Beware, in catalan cuisine,
a unique variety of tomato exists for this only purpose: the "Hanging Tomato".
Use this if you want to kiss God's hand with your mouth.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of crusty bread
  • 1 or 2 hanging tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, halved
  • 1 tbsp of extra-virgin olive oil, or to taste
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Toast the bread until browned
  2. Rub each slice of toast with garlic; this is easier to do if the peel is left on.
  3. Cut tomato(es) in half; squeesh and spread'em on the bread
  4. Dizzle with extra-virgin olive oil
  5. Sprinkle with salt and freshly ground pepper
